Talk

Realization of a "talk" service managed by a server.

The service tracks of a ready-to-talk clients list.

Clients (generally resident on different machines) acquire the track-list

only after the connection with server, then they show that list to user

so he'll be able to enstablish a connection with another user.

Linked-paired clients permits users sending and receiveng messages and

they're unvailable to other conversation istances until one stops "talking".

At the end of the conversation, clients return available until they disconnect

from server.
